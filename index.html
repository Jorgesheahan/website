A Bit About Us

Polybolt is a one-developer game stuido on a mission to create a modular First Person Shooter (FPS) for the Unreal Engine Marketplace. The vision is to design and develop individual modular components that can be seamlessly integrated by other developers into their own projects. These modules will encompass a wide array of features, including weapons systems, character controllers, AI systems, HUD and UI elements, level design tools, and multiplayer components.

Focusing on a modular development scheme means we can share our tools with the unreal engine comunity at a fraction of the price of other assets. Through thorough documentation, extensive testing, and continuous updates, the goal is to create assets that will empower other game developers to make their ideas faster and at a higher quality. The commitment to this project extends beyond development; it involves active participation in developer communities, marketing efforts to showcase the modules, and a dedication to incorporating user feedback. By leveraging the latest technologies and maintaining a user-centric approach, Polybolt aims to become a trusted name in the game development industry, contributing significantly to the creative ecosystem.
